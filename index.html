FOR HANDS & HEALTH Effective hand hygiene remains one of the best tools for preventing healthcare-associated infections, cross-infection, spread of antimicrobial resistance and in general contagious diseases. In the end efficient hand hygiene can ultimately save lives!

Desinfecting hands and hand washing are a habits that has come to stay!That's why we've put extra effort into designing a dispenser collection that is beautiful, discreet and functional.

The dispensers can be used for both disinfection sanitizer as alcohol or gel, soap, shampoo etc. depending on your needs.
